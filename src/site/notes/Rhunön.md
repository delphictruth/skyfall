# Rhunön
Rhunön is the legend [[Elves|elven]] smith, responsible for crafting every [[Rider's sword|Rider's sword]] in existence. She is the only one who knows the techniques to create them, and they have yet to be replicated by anyone else.

She is said to be ancient, from before the time of the original [[Rider Order|Rider Order]] itself.
